About Us

Hudhud Islamic College is one of the few and the leading Islamic colleges in Ethiopia. We have been committed to propagating Islamic teaching and values by carrying out our tasks with academic excellence, ethics, and a sense of responsibility.

Since our establishment, we have successfully graduated over 1,500 students. These graduates have not only learned knowledge but also the values of compassion, integrity, and service to society.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in Islamic education that nurtures future leaders.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that combines Islamic values with modern knowledge.
